Common questions

Frequently asked

How does Modern Solutions differ from a regular tenant?

We aren't an individual renter — we're a corporate tenant with a decade-long track record. We pay rent on time, every time, and our business model depends on keeping your property in excellent condition. Unlike individuals earning from a job, or Section 8 recipients, we bring corporate stability to residential leasing.

Who actually stays in my property?

Vetted traveling professionals — travel nurses, IT specialists, third-party government contractors, engineers, and corporate relocations. Every guest goes through a strict vetting process on platforms like Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, and FurnishedFinder before they stay.

What happens if something gets damaged?

We operate through platforms that provide industry-leading insurance protection (Airbnb AirCover, VRBO Guest Protection, and host platforms' damage policies). Beyond that, we take on full operational responsibility for the property and have a decade of experience resolving issues quickly and professionally.

What markets do you serve?

We currently operate across seven U.S. states with a concentration in the Northeast and Mid-Atlantic corridor. Reach out with your property's location and we'll tell you right away whether we can add it to our portfolio.

Do I have to furnish the property?

Not necessarily. Under our "We Partner" service, we'll prepare your property from the ground up — furnishing, staging, and getting it listing-ready. Under "We Rent," we can work with your existing setup if it meets short-term rental standards.

How is rent paid, and when?

Rent is paid on a fixed schedule (typically monthly, on the first) via direct deposit. You get the predictability of a long-term lease with none of the volatility of individual tenants.

What does STR Certified mean?

STR Certified is a professional credential in the short-term rental industry, signaling operators who meet strict standards for guest experience, property care, and business practices. We've held this certification as part of our commitment to professionalism.

How do I get started?

Send us a note through the form below with your property's address and a few details. We'll evaluate the fit and get back to you within two business days. If we're a match, we can typically have a new property revenue-ready within 2–4 weeks.
